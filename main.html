WELCOME

We are pleased to invite you to the International Conference on Sustainable Initiatives in the Maritime Sector: Main Challenges and Future Trends (SIM25), taking place in Oeiras, Lisboa, Portugal.


SIM25 is a multidisciplinary conference covering Maritime Logistics, Port and Supply Chain Management, Navigation Regulations, Autonomous Navigation, Decarbonization, Digitalization, and Robotics.


The program includes plenary, parallel, and poster sessions. Accepted papers from registered authors will be published in the Sustainability (Special Issue: Sustainable Initiatives within the Maritime Sector) and the Environmental Economics Journal.

SCOPE

The conference aims to gather researchers from academia and industry to present and discuss the latest trends in Sustainable Initiatives in the Maritime Sector, including but not limited to the following topics:


  • ➤ Ocean Conservation and Environmental Drivers
  • ➤ Sustainability Issues in Shipping
  • ➤ Regulations, Safety, and Autonomous Navigation
  • ➤ Education, Training, and Ocean Literacy
  • ➤ Maritime Logistic and Supply Chain Management
  • ➤ Maritime Robotics and Digital Transformation
  • ➤ Green Ports, Ships and Offshore Solutions
  • ➤ Decarbonization and Energy Innovations
